OUR VISION

To become a global Pentecostal church that is culturally relevant in vibrant evangelism, church planting, discipleship and holistic ministry.

OUR MISSION

We exist to establish responsible and self-sustaining churches filled with committed, Spirit-filled Christians of character, who will impact their communities.

ABOUT US

CHILDREN'S MINISTRY

The Children's Ministry is the children's wing of the Church and meets on Sundays before the adult service.

It trains children on biblical precepts of balanced growth. The lessons include Salvation, Worship, and Obedience, supported by games, quizzes, and "Sword Drills".

The Ministry also ministers to primary schools and orphans, organizes vacation Bible schools and camp meetings, conducts counseling sessions, organizes media evangelism, and other specialized children-related functions.

Pastor Samuel Avornyo is the current Director of the Children's Ministry. He is assisted by Elder Lawyer Alfred Aidoo. Prophet David Kankam Beditor is the Patron of the Children's Ministry.
