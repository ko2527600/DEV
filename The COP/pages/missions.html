Our Missions

Spreading the Gospel to the ends of the earth

Local Missions

Community Outreach

Reaching out to our local community through various programs, food distribution, and social services to meet the needs of our neighbors.

Healthcare Ministry

Providing medical care and health education to underserved communities in the Lapaz area and surrounding districts.

Education Support

Supporting local schools and providing educational resources, scholarships, and literacy programs for children and adults.

International Missions

African Missions

Supporting church planting and evangelism efforts across West Africa, strengthening existing churches and establishing new congregations.

Global Outreach

Sending missionaries and supporting evangelistic efforts in Europe, America, and other continents where The Church of Pentecost has presence.

Disaster Relief

Providing emergency aid and disaster relief to communities affected by natural disasters and humanitarian crises worldwide.

Mission Partners

Partner Churches

Collaborating with sister churches and denominations to maximize our evangelistic impact and share resources for kingdom work.

Mission Organizations

Working alongside established mission organizations to reach unreached people groups and support existing mission fields.

Funding Partners

Partnering with individuals, businesses, and organizations who support our mission work through financial contributions and resources.
