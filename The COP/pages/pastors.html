PASTOR

EMMANUEL OPPONG KWESI KYEREMAH

HEAD PASTOR
THE CHURCH OF PENTECOST LAPAZ DISTRICT

Pastor Emmanuel Oppong Kwesi Kyeremah serves as the Senior Pastor of The Church of Pentecost Lapaz Central, bringing years of dedicated service and spiritual leadership to our congregation. Under his guidance, our church continues to grow in faith, fellowship, and community impact.

With a deep commitment to the Word of God and a passion for pastoral care, Pastor Kyeremah leads our church family with wisdom, compassion, and unwavering faith. His ministry focuses on spiritual growth, community outreach, and nurturing the spiritual development of all members.

Pastor Kyeremah is known for his dynamic preaching, pastoral counseling, and his ability to connect with people from all walks of life. He plays a vital role in our church's mission to spread the gospel and serve our community with love and dedication.
