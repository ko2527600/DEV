Bible Study Programs

Sunday School

Every Sunday, 8:00 AM - 9:30 AM

Main Auditorium

Systematic study of God's Word for all age groups with age-appropriate materials and discussions.

Current Topics:

  • Book of Romans - Understanding Grace
  • Christian Character Development
  • Biblical Leadership Principles

Weekly Bible Study

Every Wednesday, 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM

Main Auditorium

Join us for in-depth Bible study sessions led by our experienced pastors and teachers. Each week we explore different themes and passages to strengthen our understanding of God's Word.

Current Topics:

  • The Book of Romans
  • Spiritual Gifts
  • Christian Leadership

Youth Bible Study

Every Saturday, 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM

Youth Center

Specialized Bible study sessions designed for young people. Interactive discussions, modern teaching methods, and relevant topics that address the challenges facing today's youth.

Current Topics:

  • Faith in the Digital Age
  • Building Godly Relationships
  • Purpose and Calling
