Vision 2028 Implementation

Vision Overview

Vision 2028 represents our commitment to spiritual growth, community development, and expanding our ministry impact. This comprehensive plan outlines our strategic objectives and implementation roadmap for the next phase of our church's journey.

Our vision encompasses evangelism, discipleship, leadership development, and community outreach, all aimed at fulfilling our mission to spread the gospel and serve our community effectively.

Key Objectives

  • Enhanced Worship Experience
  • Community Outreach Programs
  • Youth & Children's Ministry Growth
  • Leadership Development
  • Infrastructure Enhancement
